Therapist Profile

Darcie Dunlop

Darcie Dunlop graduated with honors from Pepperdine University and is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with a master's degree in Clinical Psychology. Prior to working in private practice, Darcie worked at Loma Linda Behavioral Medicine Center, assisting both adults and adolescents suffering from addiction, depression, and anxiety.

She specializes in addiction and recovery, as well as recovery from codependency. She has worked with couples who have experienced betrayal, and also those who have the additional stress of raising a child with special needs. She is experienced with adolescent and teen issues, including self-harm, and works with adults with high-functioning autism. She welcomes equally both straight folks and those in the LGBTQ+ community.

Darcie has recently expanded to a group practice and is now Clinical Director for the Temecula Valley Counseling and Mindfulness Center. The Center is currently hiring Licensed Mental Health workers to help meet the mental health needs of the Temecula Valley.
